Science

  • Greatly increase funding for scientific and medical research.
  • Support invention, innovation and technological development.

Education

  • Support public education and needs-based funding.
  • Return universities to the status of education and research institutions, rather than business ventures.

Health

  • Full support for Medicare and the public health system, with a focus on reducing preventable disease and improving mental health.

Environment

  • Pursue net zero emissions by 2040.
  • Embrace carbon reducing industries and technologies, and innovative recycling programs.
  • Preserve our natural resources: water, agricultural land, and native forests.
  • Build public transport systems such as high speed rail to move passengers and freight without reliance on fossil fuels.

Economy

  • Pursue an equitable tax and welfare system focused on income security and closing tax loopholes.
  • Promote competition and free enterprise to grow a better future for all.
  • Upgrade the national broadband network to world-class connection speeds.
  • Affordable childcare for all parents, to promote workforce equality.

Society

  • Recognise culture and art as fundamental parts of life.
  • Improve urban planning to ensure access to services and culture with less environmental impact

Government

  • Open, accountable and transparent government.
  • Establish a federal anti-corruption watchdog
  • Modernise the Australian constitution to establish a republic) and recognise this continent's original inhabitants.
  • Embrace secularism in all parts of government.

Freedoms

  • Establish a charter of rights.
  • Legalise euthanasia within a well-regulated scheme.
  • Decriminalise drug use to minimise harm and review drug legality.
